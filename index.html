Bio

This is Seonghwan Seo, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Chemistry, KAIST. I am currently a member of the Intelligent Chemistry Lab (ICL) Team under the supervision of Prof. Woo Youn Kim.
My focus research area is AI-based drug discovery, specifically developing deep learning models for drug design and property prediction. My other interests are the research of binding affinity, pose prediction via drug-target complex modeling.
